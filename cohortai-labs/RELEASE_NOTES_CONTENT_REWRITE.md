# Release Notes — Public Content Rewrite

This release rewrites public-facing copy to keep the brand mentor-led, premium, and trust-first.

## What changed
- Replaced internal strategy language on public pages with learner-facing, human language.
- Updated homepage proof cards, hero 3D panel copy, and section descriptions.
- Rewrote About and Courses messaging to focus on clarity, outcomes, and guidance.
- Reworked registration and review pages to remove technical/backend jargon from public view.
- Softened platform and success-story copy so it reads naturally for learners and parents.
